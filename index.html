My name is Helen Bezverhaia and I'm a senior design & business student with a passion for experimental creative direction and/in socially responsible branding.

My education began in Beedie School of Business at Simon Fraser University where I studied management information systems before discovering my passion for design that made me transfer to the School of Interactive Arts & Technology, where I focus on creative direction in branding.
Having worked in the area of public dialogue and community engagement, I gained the knowledge and appreciation for social innovation and responsible marketing (read: I want to design for good). In my free time I volunteer at a horse rescue, learn animation, and ride my bike in and around Vancouver, BC.
